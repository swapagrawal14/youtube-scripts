The Ultimate YouTube Research & Scripting Workflow

Part 1: The Foundation

A) General Script Writing Workflow

  • Hook: Grab the viewer's attention immediately.
  • Victim Introduction: Introduce the central figure.
  • Relationship Setup / Background: Provide context and establish connections.
  • Main Crime/Incident: The central event of the story.
  • Investigation Begins: The process of uncovering the truth starts.
  • Major Twist Reveal: A significant, unexpected turn in the story.
  • Motive & Planning Reveal: Explain the 'why' and 'how'.
  • Family Reaction / Emotional Impact: Show the human consequences.
  • Conclusion / Current Status: Wrap up the story and provide an update.
  • Call to Action: What you want the viewer to do next.

AI Research Prompt

Crime Story Research
**[Article Summary]:**
[Insert the link or key details of the news article here.]

**Answer the following questions based on the provided article:**

1.  **Who:** Who are the primary individuals, groups, or organizations involved? Who are the key stakeholders?
2.  **What:** What is the main event or issue? What are the critical details or developments?
3.  **Where:** Where is this event taking place, and what is its significance? How does location influence it?
4.  **When:** When did the event occur? What is the timeline? Are there historical or future implications?
5.  **Why:** Why did this event happen? What are the underlying causes, motivations, or goals?
6.  **How:** How did the event unfold? How does this event impact people, systems, or larger contexts?

Prompt AI for Data Organization

Data Structuring Prompt
I have a large amount of research data that I need to structure into a well-organized document. Your task is to:

1.  **Remove** any repetitive words, phrases, or redundant information.
2.  **Expand and explore** the key points to make the document detailed and informative.
3.  **Structure** the data into the following sections:
    *   **Introduction:** A clear, concise overview of the research (purpose, scope, significance).
    *   **Chapter 1:** Organize the first part of the research with a logical flow.
    *   **Chapter 2:** Expand on the second part, adding insights and maintaining coherence.
    *   **Chapter 3:** Conclude with a final section, summarizing key points and findings.

Ensure the writing is engaging, professional, and easy to understand. Preserve the original meaning of the data while improving readability and structure. Maintain a consistent tone throughout.
