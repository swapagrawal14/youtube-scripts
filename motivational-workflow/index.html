The Ultimate Motivational Script Workflow

Part 1: The Anatomy of Inspiration

①
Idea Research & Brainstorming Prompt

Use this prompt to lay the foundation for your video. This will help you find your core message and target audience.

Motivational Idea Research
**1. Core Message (The "Big Idea"):** What is the single, most important message I want to convey? (e.g., "Failure is not the opposite of success, it's a part of it.")

**2. Target Audience & Their Pain Point:** Who am I talking to? (e.g., Students, Entrepreneurs, Artists). What is their biggest struggle or fear right now? (e.g., Fear of starting, fear of judgment, feeling lost).

**3. Common Misconception:** What is a common belief about this topic that is wrong? (e.g., "You need to feel motivated to start." The truth is: "Action creates motivation.")

**4. Powerful Story/Metaphor:** What story, personal anecdote, or metaphor can I use to make my point unforgettable? (e.g., The story of the Chinese Bamboo Tree, Thomas Edison's failures).

**5. Actionable Framework:** What are 3-5 simple, practical steps the viewer can take to apply my message in their life? (e.g., 1. The 5-Minute Rule, 2. Set Micro-Goals, 3. Celebrate Small Wins).

Part 2: The AI-Powered Scripting Process

②
Data Structuring Prompt for AI

After brainstorming, give all your raw ideas to an AI with this prompt to structure it into a coherent narrative.

Narrative Structuring Prompt
I have brainstormed ideas for a motivational video. Your task is to structure these raw notes into a compelling narrative using the "Anatomy of Inspiration" framework.

1.  **Create a Relatable Hook:** Use the audience's pain point to create a powerful opening question or statement.
2.  **Agitate the Pain Point:** Expand on why this is a serious problem.
3.  **Create a Pivot:** Introduce the core message as a new hope or a new perspective.
4.  **Weave in the Story:** Seamlessly integrate the provided story/metaphor to support the core message.
5.  **Build the Framework:** Clearly present the actionable steps in a logical sequence.
6.  **Write an Empowering Climax:** Build towards an emotional and powerful conclusion.
7.  **Suggest an Immediate Call to Action:** Propose one small, easy first step for the viewer.
8.  **Draft a Visionary Outro:** End with a powerful, forward-looking statement.

Make the flow logical and the language engaging. Preserve the original core ideas but enhance the narrative structure.

③
Final Script Generation Prompt for AI

This is the final step. Give the structured narrative to the AI section-by-section to generate the final, polished script.

Polished Script Generation
Take the following section of a motivational narrative and expand it into a detailed, powerful video script.

**STRICT RULES:**

*   Use powerful, evocative language and emotional storytelling.
*   Add placeholders for visuals, music cues, and tone of voice. (e.g., "[Visual: Slow-motion shot of rain on a window]", "[Music: Soft piano starts to build]", "[Tone: Hopeful and rising]").
*   Expand on the ideas, do not just summarize. Dig deeper into the emotions and concepts.
*   Ensure the script flows like a real, impactful speech.
